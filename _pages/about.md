I am a PhD student in Energy Economics at the School of Economics and Management, Tsinghua University. My research focuses on electricity market dynamics, energy economics, and applied econometrics. I am supervised by Prof. Zhiqiang Mi.

## Academic Background

- **PhD in Energy Economics** (2023-present)  
  School of Economics and Management, Tsinghua University, Beijing, China  
  Supervised by Prof. Zhiqiang Mi

- **BSc in Economics** (2019-2023)  
  School of Economics and Management, Beijing Jiaotong University, Beijing, China

## Research Experience

I am currently conducting research in energy economics and applied econometrics, with a focus on electricity market dynamics. My work involves empirical analysis of market behavior using high-frequency data and energy management systems for multi-energy systems.
